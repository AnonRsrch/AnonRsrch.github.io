Study 2: Practitioner Resources

Methods

To identify resources for our analysis, all 3 authors independently searched online using Google and Twitter, and also searched for scholarly articles that discussed online visualization resources. We searched using the following keywords: "chart", "visualization", "designer", "catalog", "resource", "practitioner", "online", "model", "method", "framework", and their various combinations.
