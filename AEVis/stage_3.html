Stage 3: Field Test and Validity Evidences

Methods

In stage 3, we conducted three large-scale field tests and applied Item Analysis, Exploratory Factor Analysis, and Item Response Theory for instrument revisions, and collected validity evidences for its content, internal structure, and external structure. The primary purpose of a field test in instrument development is to collect responses for further analysis that can contribute to instrument revisions and score interpretation validations.
